DEVELOPMENT AND HEALTH OF

MY DAUGHTER

This questionnaire asks some more questions about your study child. We are
interested to know about her health and behaviour and how she gets on with other
children.

This questionnaire is like the other questionnaires you have received. To answer
simply tick the box which best describes your child or your child’s situation. Please
answer all questions that you can. If you cannot answer any question or if they do not
apply to you please put a line through them. There are no right or wrong answers.
Please just describe what happens in your situation. Your may make additional
comments at the end. All answers are confidential.

THANK YOU FOR YOUR HELP

08/12/95

          Many of the questions ask you to describe what has happened since your child was 3 -
          by this we mean since her 3rd birthday.
