The student who is named above is a member of this National Sample of 5000 young people who have been
followed up since their birth in 1946, through the generous co-operation of Local Education Authorities, schools,
school medical officers, school nurses, and of the Youth Employment Service. The present study is concemed with
the vocational training and further or technical education of those members of the sample who have left school. It is
being supported by a grant from the Department of Scientific and Industrial Research.

At a recent interview with this student, held on our behalf by a Youth Employment Officer, it was learned
