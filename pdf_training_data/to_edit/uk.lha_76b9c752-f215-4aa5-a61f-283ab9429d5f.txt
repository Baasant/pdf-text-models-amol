This questionnaire is about your health and about your family and work life. 

 

When completing the questionnaire please use a pen to circle the appropriate response to each question 

(i.e. Yes   1  ) and provide further details where requested either in boxes or in the space provided. Some 

questions don’t apply to everybody. Where you should skip questions that do not apply to you it tells you 

which question to go to next at the side of the answer you have circled (i.e. → go to Q2). Otherwise 

please continue through each question in turn. 

 

If you wish to provide further information in relation to any of the questions, please use the space 

provided at the back of the questionnaire booklet. At the end of the questionnaire you will also be asked 

to fill in a consent form that will allow us to access your hospital and GP records. If you have changed 

address recently or are about to do so, please provide your new address in the space on the back of the 

consent form. 

 

All information you give us will be treated in the strictest confidence. If you have any queries do not 

hesitate to telephone us on 020 7679 1720. 

 

When you have finished filling in the questionnaire and the consent form on page 25 please use the pre-

paid envelope provided to post it back to us. Thank you very much for your time and co-operation.
